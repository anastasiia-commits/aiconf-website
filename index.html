About

Who We Are

We are a team of leaders from Uber, Incode, and Nebius. Based in Madrid, we are deeply involved in the local and international ML and AI community — building and shaping real-world machine learning products for 9+ years.

Who This Conference is For

AI/ML engineers, Data scientists, Researchers in STEM, Top graduate students with real math/coding/ML experience, Mid-level and senior engineers looking for practice and peer exchange

Why We Do It

Most conferences talk about AI without showing how it’s actually built. We want to create a space for engineers and researchers to discuss practical solutions, real problems, and the approaches that they use.

What to Expect

Hands-on workshops and Applied AI Talks led by engineers from teams such as OpenAI, NVIDIA, Perplexity, Nebius, Google DeepMind, and more. The sessions are designed to share best practices, and advanced approaches.

When & Where

Join us in sunny Barcelona at a modern, thoughtfully designed venue — on May 16, 2026.

Program

A focused, engineer-first day built around applied frontier AI — how to build and ship large language models and computer vision in production, plus best practices for AI-assisted coding.

Hands-on Workshops & Technical Walkthroughs

Practical, step-by-step sessions. Topics include:

  • AI-assisted coding workflows
  • Integrating LLMs & VLMs into real products
  • Vision models for deepfake detection

Applied AI Talks

Focused on mechanisms, architectures, and implementation details:

LLMs

  • Training LLMs
  • Building coding agents
  • Using AI for coding
  • AI agents for applications (energy, manufacturing, healthcare)
  • Multimodal LLMs

Computer Vision

  • GenAI in 2D & 3D
  • Deepfake detection
  • AI for Robotics
  • Building AI-first startups

Networking

Conversations that lead to real ideas and solutions — with peers who build.

How to Get to the Venue

✈️ From the Airport

Barcelona–El Prat Airport (BCN) is ~20–30 minutes from the city center. You can reach the venue by Aerobús, metro, or taxi.

🚇 Public Transport

Barcelona has an extensive metro and bus network. Exact metro and bus stops will be shared closer to the event.

🚶 On Foot / Taxi

The venue is located in a central, walkable area. Taxis and ride-sharing services are widely available.
